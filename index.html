About Us

There are ways to dissect what Bicu Lounge is, has been, and will
be, though nothing like allowing it to rule your night will have you
understand why it's 'it'.
When BICU landed in Kigali in 2021, it acted the part. The most
uninterrupted views of the city's skyline were your MonaLisa to
stare at as you danced. The lofty lounge then became home to
where cocktails and bites were finer than wine.
As for the shows, not only are they still magnificent but have
become more unique and galvanizing than you remember them.
Now isn't that and experience?
